From January 20 to January 25, China National Music Theatre will launch "Feel the charm of traditional music", an exclusive performance from the National Traditional Orchestra. The performance selects five classical traditional folk music pieces reflecting different regional characteristics: 紫竹调 (Zi zhu diao), which embodies the humanitic culture of the south of the Yangtze River, 金沙滩 (Jin sha tan), which combines the culture of Qin opera, 梅花三弄 (Mei hua san nong), which is adapted from the ancient Qin music, the most famous folk song 梁祝 (Liangzhu) and 禅寺钟鼓 (Chan si zhong gu) showing the characteristics of percussion. The emotions of the five tracks are quite different, and will show the development achievements of traditional music in an all-round and multi angle, reflect the development vein of Chinese traditionl music, promote the prosperity of contemporary traditional music through this performance, let the audience be in the charm of traditional music.
